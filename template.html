Description Pop Up Title

  • Smith's pricing is an important part of a Trader's daily life. Understanding the differences and opportunities based on pricing strategies gives you various means for improving Smith's position in trades. This course will introduce you to all of Smith's pricing and help you to start your understanding of how to leverage different positions and trade opportunities.
  • Some questions to think about when considering pricing include:
    • What is important to consider in market pricing?
    • What is NOT usually important?
  • Smith's pricing types include the following:
    • Direct Pricing
    • Franchised Pricing
    • Independent or Market Pricing
    • Standard or Contract Price
    • Price Variance (Purchase Price Variance (PPV))
    • Lead Time
    • Target Price
    • TTA or Wire in Advance
    • TTA or Wire upon Receipt
    • Pro Forma Invoice
    • Net Terms
    • Falling Down
    • Gross Profit
    • Profit Margin & Markup
    • Non-Cancellable & Non-Returnable (NCNR)
    • Date Code
    • Date Code Restriction
